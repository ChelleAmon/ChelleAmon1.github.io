Filipino Desserts

Being an archipelago with different regions, the Philippines offers a wide variety of specialty desserts
made primarily of rice, coconut and fruits that are widespread in this tropical country.
With agriculture being their main source of livelihood, rice has been a staple food of the Philippines.
Because of this, rice is not only eaten with dishes but is also an ingredient in a lot of Filipino desserts.
Some types of dessert call for rice flour while others call for glutinous or sticky rice.
