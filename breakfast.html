Filipino Breakfast

People say breakfast is the most important meal of the day.
Well, for Filipinos, no one will argue about it.
A typical heavy filipino breakfast consists of fried rice,
sunny side-up egg, and a choice of meat viands.
Of course, filipino breakfast is not complete without the barako coffee.

Breakfast Recipes:

  • Tapsilog (Fried rice with egg and beef tapa)
  • Longsilog (Fried rice with egg and longganisa)
  • Champorado
  • Arrozcaldo
  • Kapeng Barako (Barako coffee)
